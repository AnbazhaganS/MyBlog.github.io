About me

Welcome to AbaqusModelingTips. My name is Subramani Anbazhagan. Currently, I'm a researcher at Energy Research Institute @ NTU (ERIAN), where I joined after masters in Aerospace with TUM-NTU. At ERIAN, I support design and analysis of wind turbine structures (Mainly rotor blades).

I'm a nascent researcher mainly focusing on fibre reinforced composites and structural analysis (Primarily Abaqus). Picking up the analysis skills for the past few years from trainings with DSS (Awesome!), webinars and documentation. Besides research, I also enjoy programming (Python), which I picked up recently; Thanks to edX [6.00.1x & 6.00.2x].

Other than Research, Analysis, and Programming, I love cars and Formula 1; Of course, who wouldn't want their Adrenalin pumped to the rhythm of the V6!!

I will post few tips/hacks and tricks here, which learn or find useful when I stumble upon them. You can contact me at m130148@e.ntu.sg or at Facebook.

Thanks for reading; Cheers!
